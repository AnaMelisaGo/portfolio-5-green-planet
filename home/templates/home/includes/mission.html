" Hand in hand we can help save our planet and help those who really want to enjoy a nice good meal on their plate without spending too much. And of course, save your business effort and money on food production. Nothing should be wasted. "
Food waste

Stop for a moment and take a look at your own trash bin. Loads of food waste are thrown away. Mainly because nobody totally consumed them or it is nearly at the point of expiration.

Left over

Restaurants, bakeries, hotels, grocery stores etc. produce food more than people could consume. It's a total waste of money, time and effort on the business part.

Save food, save the planet

This is where we come into action. Our main goal is to help our planet by reducing food waste. To do so we rescue food that is still consumable. Because we believe that left overs are not meant to the thrash but rather to be enjoyed and be consumed.

Low cost food

We strive to offer good promotions to everyone, especially those who like good food, low cost products that aren't totally consumed or nearly about to expire. It is the best way to taste food from stores or restaurants.

Great discounts

We guarantee discounts on goods and food even more than half the price of its total cost (up to 70% off), and some stuff seem absolutely free. It's a good money saver for those who have a low budget monthly.
